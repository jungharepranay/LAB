VIT Projects Overview

Software Development Projects (SDP)

SDP focuses on building software solutions using advanced programming and development techniques. These projects are aligned with real-world industrial requirements and emphasize collaborative development.

Engineering Design and Innovation (EDI)

EDI highlights innovative engineering solutions, prototyping, and creative problem-solving approaches. These projects foster critical thinking and emphasize interdisciplinary collaboration.

Design Thinking (DT)

DT emphasizes understanding user needs and creating human-centered solutions. These projects leverage ideation and iteration to address complex challenges creatively.

Course Projects

Course Projects integrate theoretical knowledge with practical applications. They are tailored to specific subjects and help students master core concepts through hands-on learning.
